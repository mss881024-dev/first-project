Who We Are

A Press Built on Conviction

Founded in Edinburgh in 1987, Meridian Press began as a single editor's wager: that readers hunger for books of genuine literary ambition, and that those books deserve to be published with care and kept in print for decades.

We remain independently owned. We do not answer to shareholders. Every title on our list is there because someone in this building believed in it unreservedly.

For Writers

Submissions

We read every submission we receive. We respond within eight weeks. We will tell you honestly whether your work belongs with us, and we will treat your manuscript with the attention it deserves.

What we publish

  • Literary fiction of any length, including novellas
  • Essay collections with a sustained intellectual or personal focus
  • Poetry collections — we read them whole, not in excerpts
  • Narrative non-fiction with strong original reporting
  • Literary translation from any language
  • First books from writers of evident ambition

What we do not publish

  • Genre fiction: thriller, romance, fantasy, science fiction
  • Self-help, lifestyle, or wellness titles
  • Children's or young adult literature
  • Celebrity memoir or platform-driven non-fiction
  • Illustrated books or graphic novels
  • Previously self-published work
